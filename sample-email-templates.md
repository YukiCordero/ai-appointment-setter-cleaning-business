# Sample Email Templates

These are the portfolio-safe templates used by the workflow. Replace the business name, booking link, sender address, and tone before using in production.

## Hot Lead: Booking Email

**Subject:** Book Your Cleaning Service Appointment

Hi {{first_name}},

Thank you for reaching out to {{business_name}}.

We received your request for {{service_needed}}{{location_phrase}}. Based on your preferred schedule, your request looks time-sensitive, so you can book your cleaning appointment here:

{{calendar_booking_link}}

After you choose a time, our team will review your details and confirm the appointment.

Best,  
{{business_name}} Team

---

## Warm Lead: Qualifying Email

**Subject:** Quick Question About Your Cleaning Request

Hi {{first_name}},

Thank you for contacting {{business_name}}.

We received your request for {{service_needed}}{{location_phrase}}. To recommend the best cleaning option, could you please confirm your preferred cleaning date and approximate property size?

Once we have those details, we can help you choose the right service.

Best,  
{{business_name}} Team

---

## Cold Lead: Nurture Email

**Subject:** Thanks for Contacting {{business_name}}

Hi {{first_name}},

Thank you for reaching out to {{business_name}}.

We received your cleaning service inquiry. When you are ready, you can reply to this email with more details about your preferred schedule, property type, and cleaning needs.

We’ll be happy to help.

Best,  
{{business_name}} Team

---

## Manual Review Acknowledgment

**Subject:** We Received Your Cleaning Request

Hi {{first_name}},

Thank you for contacting {{business_name}}.

We received your inquiry, but our team needs to review a few details before sending the best next step. We will check your request and follow up shortly.

Best,  
{{business_name}} Team
