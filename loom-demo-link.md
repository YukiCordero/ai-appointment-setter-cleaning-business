## Suggested 90-second demo script

1. Show the n8n canvas and explain the flow: webhook → normalize lead → score lead → route by Hot/Warm/Cold/Manual Review.
2. Submit one test lead using the sample payload in `README.md`.
3. Show the generated lead score and email branch.
4. Show the Google Sheets tabs receiving the lead, CRM row, and follow-up task.
5. Show the Google Calendar event being created when `preferred_schedule` is parseable.
6. End with the business value: faster response time, cleaner lead tracking, and less manual follow-up work.
